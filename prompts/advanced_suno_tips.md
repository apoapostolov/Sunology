## Context

### Advanced Suno Prompting Techniques

#### Instrumentation Specificity
- Specify exact instruments and their roles in the mix
- Use technical terms: 'distorted electric guitar solo', 'fingerpicked acoustic guitar'
- Mention microphone types and recording techniques when relevant

#### Production Style Details
- Reference specific production eras: '1960s wall of sound'
- Mention mixing techniques: 'reverb-heavy mix', 'dry, punchy drums'
- Specify vocal processing: 'double-tracked harmonies', 'auto-tune chorus'

#### Genre Fusion Techniques
- Combine unexpected elements: 'prog rock complexity with pop accessibility'
- Use hybrid terminology: 'electronic folk', 'orchestral hip-hop'
- Do not reference real artist names; describe genre, era, and technique only.

#### Emotional Arc Descriptions
- Describe how the instrumentation should evolve with the song's emotional journey
- Use dynamic markings: 'building from intimate acoustic verses to explosive choruses'
- Specify tempo changes and their emotional impact

#### Cultural and Historical Context
- Reference specific time periods, regions, or cultural movements
- Mention traditional instruments from specific cultures
- Use historically accurate instrumentation for period pieces

## Instructions

Apply these advanced prompting techniques to create more detailed and effective Suno AI prompts. Focus on specific instrumentation, production techniques, and emotional arcs. Avoid real artist names in style prompts.
