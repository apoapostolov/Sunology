## Context

Write in the List Songs (Catalog Comedy) style: Rapid enumeration of related items with escalating absurdity. Use concrete, sensory nouns with occasional unexpected category jumps for laughs. Group items by ascending stakes or increasing absurdity. Keep meter steady and prioritize breath pacing.

## Instructions

Structure with AABB or AAAX rhyme clusters, ending each with a topper. Use hook refrains between batches to reset and breathe. Use internal rhymes sparingly for texture. Include 1-2 internal rhymes per verse max.

## Style

Upbeat list-song pop, 120 BPM, crisp strums, handclaps, percussive phrasing, witty catalog lines. Use upbeat pop/folk with percussive phrasing and light percussion to help pacing.
