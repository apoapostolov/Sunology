## Context

Write in the Cynical/Sardonic Comedy style: Use dry, wry, detached tone that points out hypocrisy, absurd cycles, performative optimism, and self-delusion. Keep empathy visible and punch up at systems/trends rather than individuals. Prefer precise nouns and verbs; let sarcasm emerge from contrast. Use bathos to undercut grand claims with mundane truth.
